Our current time display is highly accurate and updates every second based on your device's system clock. It automatically detects your timezone to ensure the time shown is correct for your location.

How are week numbers calculated? +

Week numbers follow the ISO 8601 international standard. According to this system, week 1 is the week with the year's first Thursday, which means the first week always contains January 4th. This ensures consistency in international business and planning.

Can I calculate dates more than 180 days in the future? +

Currently, we display future dates up to 180 days ahead for optimal performance and readability. For dates beyond this range, you can use our interactive calendar to navigate to future months and years.

How does the leap year calculation work? +

Leap years occur every 4 years to account for the fact that Earth's orbit around the Sun takes approximately 365.25 days. However, years divisible by 100 are not leap years, unless they are also divisible by 400. This adjustment keeps our calendar aligned with astronomical seasons.
